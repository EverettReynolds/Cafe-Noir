About Us

Java Noir was born out of a desire for the best cup of coffee.
How many times have you said to yourself that you wanted a fantastic cup of coffee, only to have it come out tasting, unlike what you wanted?
Whether it be that it’s too watery, not strong enough, or doesn’t have the right whole-bodied flavor, we have you covered.
Java Noir was created to help every person have the best-individualized coffee experience and the best-brewed coffee possible.
Born out of a coffee drinker in 2019 not wanting to take the downfalls of coffee as they were, he strived to integrate his love of coffee with his creativity for technology.
Located in midtown Atlanta, Java Noir, nestled in a city busy with the travel of many, is a hub for big business.
Every man and woman get the treatment of coffee.
They deserve to fuel their day when they are hard at work.
We have been serving since 2019 from the humble beginnings of a college student with an idea.
We proudly operate our business with maximum enjoyment for our consumers and strive to this perfection every day we work.
